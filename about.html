TigerCycle is a resource for RIT dorm residents to see which washing machines and dryers are available in each laundry room. Users can also report issues with any machine to both RIT and our list of Unresolved Issues on the "Reviews" page. Users can use that page to see which machines are broken and haven't received maintenance yet.
