The Program


TigerCycle is a resource for RIT dorm residents to see which washing machines and dryers are available in each laundry room. Users can also report issues with machines on the "Reviews" page. They can also issue maintenance requests to RIT through MyLife. On the "Useful Links" page, users can gain access to third-party and RIT resources.



Our Team


Comprised of four young college students, Wyatt Holcombe, Ben Stevenson, Gabe Knapp, and Jake Lombardo, all produced this program. Ben originally came up with the idea to create TigerCycle and the boys all unanimously agreed that we should make this program. It has been a semester long project and a long time coming and we are proud to be able to present it to you!
